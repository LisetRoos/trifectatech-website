Trifecta Tech Foundation

Trifecta Tech Foundation is a non-profit and a Public Benefit Organisation (Dutch: ANBI), the equivalent of a 501(c)(3) organisation in the USA.

We develop and maintain digital commons, open-source software and open standards for vital systems.
