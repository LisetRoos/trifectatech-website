What we do

We develop and maintain open-source software and open standards for vital infrastructure in the public interest. Pendulum is the most prominent project of Trifecta Tech Foundation. The Pendulum project is building modern, open-source implementations of the Network Time Protocol and the Precision Time Protocol.
